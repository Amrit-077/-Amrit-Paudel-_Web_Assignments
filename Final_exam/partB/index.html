Our Menu

Chicken

Our signature chicken dishes are prepared with free-range poultry and a blend of secret spices. Try our famous Lemon-Herb Roasted Chicken with rosemary-infused potatoes or the spicy Buffalo Wings with homemade blue cheese dip. All chicken dishes are cooked to perfection - juicy on the inside with a crispy golden skin.

All our chicken is sourced from local farms that practice ethical farming. We offer gluten-free and dairy-free options for most chicken dishes upon request.

Popular choices include:

  • Classic Fried Chicken
  • Honey Garlic Chicken
  • Spicy Peri-Peri Chicken

Beef

Premium aged Angus beef is the star of our beef selection. The 12oz Ribeye Steak, dry-aged for 21 days, is a customer favorite. For a truly unique experience, try our Beef Wellington wrapped in flaky puff pastry.

All beef dishes come with your choice of two sides from our seasonal selection. We offer cooking temperature options from rare to well-done.

Specialty beef dishes:

  • Prime Rib Dinner
  • Filet Mignon
  • Braised Short Ribs

Sushi

Experience authentic Japanese sushi prepared by our master chef with daily fresh ingredients. Our Dragon Roll with eel and avocado is legendary, or try the Chef's Special Nigiri selection featuring seasonal fish.

We offer traditional sushi as well as creative fusion rolls that combine Japanese techniques with local flavors. All sushi is prepared with sushi-grade fish and organic rice.

Customer favorites:

  • Rainbow Roll
  • Spicy Tuna Roll
  • Unagi Nigiri
